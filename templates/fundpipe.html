Fundpipe



1. Open a fundpipe and start a call for funds by sharing the link to your pipe

2. Every fund will be split. Half will go to your project. Half will be distributed to all previous funders.

3. The funders of your project will get returns while funding is still active. Early funders will receive more returns.

4. You can refund to your pipe. Refunds will be distributed to all funders.

5. A pipe can be closed by refunding all previous funds. Every pipe can stay open indefinitely.

No fund is guaranteed to be partially or fully returned.
You are not committed to partially or fully return any of the funds.
