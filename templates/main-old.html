Fundpipe



1. Open a fundpipe and start a call for funds. Share your fundpipe link.

2. Every fund will be split. Half will go to your project. Half will be distributed proportionally to all previous funders.

3. The funders of your project will get returns while funding is still active. Early funders will receive more returns.

4. You can refund to your pipe. Refunds will be distributed proportionally to all funders.

5. A pipe can be closed by refunding all previous funds. Every pipe can stay open indefinitely.



All funds are considered risk investments in your project.

You are not committed to partially or fully return any of the funds.
